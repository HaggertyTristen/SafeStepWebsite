The Critical Threat: Landmines

Global Mine Contamination

Over 60 countries remain contaminated by landmines. Current detection methods are manual, slow, and force personnel into life-threatening situations.

  • Manual Detection: Dangerous and time-consuming
  • Field Casualties: Preventable deaths among military and humanitarian personnel
  • Slow Recovery: Landmine contamination delays post-conflict rebuilding

Humanitarian and Military Impact

Landmines continue to kill or injure thousands every year. Clearing them is hazardous, and traditional methods cannot scale quickly enough to save lives.

  • Military EOD units and combat engineers face high-risk operations
  • NGO demining and post-conflict civilian teams are vulnerable
