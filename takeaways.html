Key Takeaways & Roadmap

Key Insights

  • SafeStep significantly reduces field casualties compared to manual detection.
  • AlexNet achieves high accuracy in real-time terrain classification.
  • Wearable AI integration enables immediate haptic and visual alerts.
  • Scalable design allows mass production under $400/unit.

Roadmap to Deployment

  1. Integrate additional sensors: infrared, metal detection, ground-penetrating radar.
  2. Expand dataset with diverse terrains and climates worldwide.
  3. Optimize edge computing for faster real-time alerts.
  4. Test with military and humanitarian field operations.

Global Impact

With proper funding and continued development, SafeStep can save lives worldwide and enable rapid post-conflict recovery for contaminated regions.
