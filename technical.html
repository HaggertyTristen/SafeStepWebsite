Technical Approach

AlexNet

AlexNet was used as the baseline CNN. The convolutional layers were frozen to retain pretrained feature extraction, while the classifier (fully connected layers) was retrained to predict “mine” and “safe.” Training utilized the Adam optimizer with cross-entropy loss for five epochs. Hyperparameters included batch sizes of 16, 32, and 64, and learning rates of 0.001 and 0.0001. Data augmentation, including rotations, flips, and brightness adjustments, was applied to improve model generalization.

ResNet18

ResNet18 was trained similarly, with frozen convolutional layers and a retrained fully connected layer for binary classification. Model performance was evaluated on both training and validation sets using accuracy and loss metrics.

Evaluation Metrics

  • Accuracy on training and validation sets
  • Loss curves during training
  • Confusion matrices to measure classification performance
