Discussion & Takeaways

  • Freezing convolutional layers preserved pretrained feature extraction, reducing training time.
  • Data augmentation improved validation accuracy and model robustness.
  • ResNet18 slightly outperformed AlexNet in overall classification accuracy.
  • Batch size and learning rate influenced convergence speed and final performance.

Recommendations

  • Test additional CNN architectures such as VGG16 or EfficientNet for comparison.
  • Increase dataset size or add synthetic images to improve generalization.
  • Consider fine-tuning convolutional layers for further performance improvement.
