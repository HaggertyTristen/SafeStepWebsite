SafeStep: AI-Powered Landmine Detection

Landmines remain a significant humanitarian threat worldwide, causing injuries and fatalities every year. SafeStep leverages deep learning to classify terrain as “mine” or “safe,” offering a safer and more efficient detection method.

Key Motivations

  • Prevent civilian injuries and fatalities.
  • Enable faster, safer landmine detection.
  • Leverage AI to reduce human risk.
