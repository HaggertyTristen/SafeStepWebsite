Problem & Motivation

SafeStep is an AI-powered solution designed to detect wet or slippery surfaces in real time using image classification. This can help reduce accidents and improve safety in public and private spaces like hospitals, malls, and workplaces.

Slips and falls account for millions of injuries annually. By automating detection using a trained CNN (AlexNet), we aim to give environments a “smart safety check.”

Dataset

Our dataset consists of two primary classes: Safe (dry) and Mine (wet/slippery). Images are separated into train and test folders for model evaluation.
