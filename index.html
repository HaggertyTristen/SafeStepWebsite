About SafeStep

SafeStep is a wearable AI detection system integrated into smart boots and ankle devices. Using computer vision and deep learning with AlexNet, SafeStep identifies mines before your next step, delivering instant vibration and visual alerts to keep operators safe.

Over 60 countries remain contaminated by landmines. Current detection methods are slow and dangerous. SafeStep dramatically accelerates detection while reducing field casualties, supporting both military EOD units and humanitarian demining operations.
