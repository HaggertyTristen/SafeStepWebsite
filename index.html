Protecting Lives One Step at a Time

Over 60 countries remain contaminated by landmines, leaving millions at risk. SafeStep is a wearable AI-powered detection system designed to protect operators in both military and humanitarian mine-clearance operations.

Why SafeStep Matters

  • 60+ countries: Active mine threats worldwide
  • Manual Detection: Dangerous, slow, and error-prone
  • Field Casualties: Thousands of preventable deaths annually
  • Immediate Alerts: Haptic and visual warnings delivered in real-time

Primary Users

  • Military EOD units
  • Elite bomb disposal specialists
  • Combat engineers
  • Humanitarian NGO demining organizations

SafeStep Vision

SafeStep combines cutting-edge computer vision and wearable hardware to detect buried explosives before a step is taken, reducing risk and accelerating detection across military and civilian operations. Future expansions include sensor fusion with infrared, metal detection, and ground-penetrating radar.
