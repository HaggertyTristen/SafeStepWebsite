SafeStep

AI-Powered Landmine Detection

Problem & Motivation

Every year, thousands of soldiers and civilians are injured or killed by hidden landmines and unexploded ordnance (UXOs). Current detection systems rely heavily on drones or handheld sensors, leaving soldiers vulnerable in uncertain terrain. SafeStep aims to change that — a wearable AI system that detects landmines before a soldier’s next step.

Our Mission

SafeStep combines machine learning, computer vision, and real-time field awareness to make every step safer. Our goal is simple: save lives through intelligent detection.
