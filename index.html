SafeStep: Wearable AI Landmine Detection System

Problem & Motivation

Landmines and unexploded ordnance (UXOs) continue to endanger millions of people in post-conflict regions worldwide. Despite advancements in robotics and detection systems, traditional solutions remain costly, bulky, or inaccessible to local populations.

SafeStep is a wearable AI-powered detection system that uses computer vision and deep learning to identify hidden mines before a person takes a step. The goal is to provide a lightweight, real-time, and affordable solution for humanitarian demining.

With over 60 countries still contaminated by landmines, and thousands of civilian injuries annually, the urgency for an accessible detection technology is clear. SafeStep aims to bridge this gap with artificial intelligence.
